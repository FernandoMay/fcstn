description Abstract

This research introduces the Fractal Cognitive Space-Time Network (FCSTN), a novel framework for synthesizing immersive neuro-digital environments. By leveraging recursive geometry and asynchronous neural data stream synchronization (NDSS), we achieve near-zero perceptual latency in hyper-complex virtual constructs. Our methodology integrates fractal-based level-of-detail management with geodesic cognitive mapping to optimize cognitive load during high-fidelity digital interaction.
